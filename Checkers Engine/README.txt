Make sure you keep all files in the same directory! (.dll files are required for the program to run)

Click on a piece to select it, then click on a square to move it. If you want to deselect a piece, click on it again.
To pause the game, press the pause button on the right. To resume, press the play button.
Use the boxes on the left to change the AI's difficulty. Note that the AI will take longer to make decisions as the difficulty increases.
